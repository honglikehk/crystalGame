Crystal Collection:

Instructions: There are four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. You will be shown a random number at the start of the game. The goal of the game is to click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to to your total score. You win if your total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. You lose if your score goes above the random number.
